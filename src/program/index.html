CALL FOR PROPOSALS

It's time for Rubyists to come share their Ruby knowledge with their peers through the ME.RubyConf CFP. If you are interested in submitting a talk, please click 'Submit a talk'. There you will find details on how to submit a proposal, what we're looking for, and all you'll receive as a speaker. Thanks!

SCHEDULE

You have until 15:00 GMT on Feb 10th to get your talk in, though we highly encourage you to submit early as we'll be accepting some talks early.

POLICIES

Please note that while everyone is expected to comply with our Conference Policies , speakers in particular should take care to craft presentations that fit within our anti-harassment policies. If you have any questions or concerns about the material that you're proposing, please mention them when submitting
