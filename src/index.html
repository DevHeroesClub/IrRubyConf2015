What is ME.RubyConf ?

ME.RubyConf is the largest annual gathering for the community in Iran and Middle East using and developing the open-source Ruby programming language. ME.RubyConf is organized by the IranOnRails community for the community. We try to keep registration far cheaper than most comparable technology conferences in order to keep ME.RubyConf accessible to the widest group possible.

Cool, How can I come to ME.RubyConf?

You have to buy a ticket to participate in ME.RubyConf. We are trying hard to keep ME.RubyConf tickets cheap and affordable by every one.
